Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer
  • 4 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Vegitable (chopped)
  • Cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring to fry paneer . Drain oil and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and onion and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add the red pepper flakes and a pinch of salt. Stir for about 30 seconds.
